Essential Travel Information

Best Time to Visit

  • October - March: Pleasant weather, ideal for sightseeing (15-25°C)
  • April - June: Hot season, good for hill stations (25-42°C)
  • July - September: Monsoon, waterfalls at their best

How to Reach

  • By Air: Birsa Munda Airport, Ranchi (Direct flights from major cities)
  • By Train: Ranchi, Dhanbad, Bokaro stations well connected
  • By Road: National highways connect to Delhi, Kolkata, Patna

Where to Stay

  • Luxury: 5-star hotels in Ranchi, Jamshedpur
  • Mid-range: Government guest houses, forest lodges
  • Budget: Tribal homestays, eco-resorts

Safety Tips

  • Carry valid ID for forest area visits
  • Book safaris and treks through authorized operators
  • Respect tribal customs and photography guidelines
  • Use insect repellent in forest areas
